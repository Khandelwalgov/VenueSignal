# Synthetic Data Strategy

**Disclosure**
> Unity Stadium is a synthetic venue created for demonstrating operational incident reasoning, accessibility-impact analysis, and constrained route recovery. It is not an official FIFA venue map. All crowd telemetry, asset status, and operational events are synthetic or evaluator-supplied.

## Evaluator-Input Strategy
The product relies on evaluator-supplied operational events (e.g. lift breakages) and synthetic crowd telemetry to simulate real-world operations. 

## Prohibited Claims
The repository does not claim any official affiliation with FIFA or use any proprietary real stadium floor plans, private API integrations, or real tracking systems.
